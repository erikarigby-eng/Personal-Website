📚 My Journey

📝 Writing in the Rain

Hi my name is Erika Rigby. I've always loved the feeling of getting lost in a good book — but I also know what it's like to rush through school reading just for a grade. That gap between joy and obligation is what inspired my journey.

I'm writing curriculum-ready fiction that teens actually want to read, and building a BookTube-style platform where students can share reviews, connect by chapter, and discover stories that feel alive.

My mission is simple: spark a global teen reading revolution and help more students fall back in love with books. For me, this is about more than publishing a novel — it's about showing that reading can be exciting, relevant, and unforgettable.
